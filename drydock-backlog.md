Mac-only by design — matches Apple Notes constraint. Store the selection rationale on the run row so TaskCard can render it ("routed to Claude — 72% cap remaining vs. Gemini 12%"). Degrade gracefully to current round-robin when no fresh snapshot is available. | P2 | moderate | M | Maximises use of paid-tier headroom before hitting overage; useful when one account has rolled over and another is nearly capped. | Not Started |
